## Current Empirical Readout

- Dataset: NBER Dehejia-Wahba NSW treated sample plus PSID controls.
- Rows: 2,675.
- Baseline PSM ATT: 1,461.2030.
- Baseline PSM 95% CI: -209.7343 to 3,132.1403.
- Raw-scale robustness surface: 11.7% positive estimates, 23.3% CI-crosses-zero share.

Professional interpretation: under this tested Wiki C specification space, the paper-like
positive matching baseline is not stable across the broader matching, weighting, and
outcome-model grid. This is a specification-robustness result on public data, not an
independent repeat-study conclusion or final scientific adjudication.
